WHY CSC?

Changsha is a vibrant city in central China that offers a perfect blend of history, culture, natural beauty, and economic growth. With ancient landmarks, modern attractions, and delicious cuisine, Changsha is a must-visit destination. Whether you are the vistors, professionals or investors you'll find your interests and values HERE!

History

Changsha is a city in China with a history of over 3,000 years. It was a center of agriculture, commerce, and culture during the Han and Tang dynasties. It played a significant role in the Chinese Revolution and the Second Sino-Japanese War. Today, it is a major city known for its cultural heritage, natural scenery, and economic development.

Culture

Changsha's culture is a rich and diverse blend of ancient traditions and modern influences. From museums and galleries to traditional arts and literature, the city offers a unique cultural experience. Additionally, Changsha's spicy Hunan cuisine is famous for its bold flavors and fiery spices.

Climate

Changsha, the capital of Hunan province in China, enjoys a humid subtropical climate with four distinct seasons. The mild winters and comfortable temperatures in spring and autumn make these seasons ideal for outdoor activities and sightseeing. Despite the hot and humid summers, the abundant rainfall contributes to the city's lush greenery and scenic landscapes.

Economy

Changsha, a thriving economic hub in China's Hunan province, boasts a diverse and robust economy. Key advantages include its well-developed infrastructure, strategic location in the Yangtze River Delta region, and a strong focus on high-tech industries, education, and research. These factors contribute to the city's rapid economic growth and its attractiveness to both domestic and international investors.
