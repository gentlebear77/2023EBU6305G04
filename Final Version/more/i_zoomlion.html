Zoomlion Smart Industrial City

Zoomlion is a Chinese construction machinery and equipment manufacturer and the only heavy industry enterprise in China listed in Shanghai and Hong Kong. It is mainly engaged in the development and manufacture of major equipment required for construction, energy, transportation, environmental protection, agricultural and disaster relief purposes. Founded in 1992 as the Changsha Construction Machinery Research Institute in Beijing in 1956, it became the sixth largest construction machinery manufacturer and the largest concrete machinery manufacturer in the world in 2008. By 2012, Zoomlion had achieved an average compound annual growth rate of over 65%, making it the fastest growing construction machinery company in the world.
