Favorable policies for investors

1. Optimising the business environment:

Optimising the business environment, simplifying the administrative approval process, reducing investment costs and improving efficiency.


2. Industrial support policies:

Implement industrial support policy for key areas and provide various preferential measures.


3. Innovation and entrepreneurship support policy:

Provide support for technological innovation and entrepreneurial projects to encourage innovative and entrepreneurial activities.


4. Financial support policy:

Support the development of quality projects and SMEs through financial policy guidance and loan guarantees to reduce financing costs.




Gross Domestic Product in Changsha



   Changsha City is the capital of Hunan Province and has a relatively fast economic development rate. During the period of 2017-2022, Changsha's GDP continued to grow with relatively balanced development of various industries. 2022 Changsha's GDP is expected to grow by 4.8%, ranking among the top trillion cities in China; the value added of large-scale industries grew by 8.5%, ranking first in central China and among the top in the country; local general public budget revenue exceeded 120 billion RMB, showing a good trend of steady progress in economic development.
