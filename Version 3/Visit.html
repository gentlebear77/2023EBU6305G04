Top Attractions


Yuelu Mountain

Yuelu Mountain is one of China's renowned historical and cultural mountains. The mountain is home to many famous historical sites, such as the Yuelu Academy and Aiwan Pavilion. Yuelu Academy is one of the four most prestigious academies in Chinese history, boasting a long history and profound cultural heritage.

Yuelu Mountain

Orange Island

Orange Island is a popular tourist destination in Changsha. It is named for its location opposite Yuelu Mountain. The statue of young Mao Zedong on Orange Island is one of the city's landmarks, and the island also hosts grand events such as the Orange Island Fireworks Festival.

Orange Island

Hunan Provincial
Museum

The Hunan Provincial Museum is an important cultural institution that showcases the history and art of Hunan Province. The museum houses a variety of precious relics, including the well-preserved Mawangdui Han Dynasty Tombs and their stunning artifacts, such as the exquisite silk paintings and the well-preserved ancient female corpse.

Hunan Provincial Museum





Entertainment & Shopping


Changsha is home to many large shopping centres, department stores and distinctive
neighbourhoods. Wuyi Square, Huangxing Road Pedestrian Street and Pozi Street are
some of the liveliest shopping areas in downtown Changsha, with a variety of brand
name shops, special snacks and traditional handicrafts.

Entertainment in Changsha is varied and includes cinemas, KTV, bars and shows of all
kinds. Changsha's nightlife is also quite rich, especially the bar street in the Hexi area.
Traditional cultural events such as the Xiangjiang Theatre and the Orange Island Fireworks
Gala in Changshaare also not to be missed.







Gourmet Food



With Hunan cuisine at its core, Changsha cuisine is a rich blend of spicy, numbing, aromatic, fresh and sour flavours that showcase a strong local identity. It offers a wide variety of cuisines, including street food, home-cooked dishes and specialties. Visitors to Changsha can sample a variety of delicious Hunan dishes and savour authentic snacks in the night markets and food streets to experience the rich food culture and unique dining experience of Changsha.

Accommodation


Yuelu District

Located in the western part of Changsha, Yuelu District is home to many famous attractions such as Yuelu Mountain, Yuelu Academy and Orange Island. Choosing to stay in the Yuelu District will make it easy for visitors to explore these attractions.




Tianxin District

The Tianxin District is the downtown area of Changsha and has many shopping centres, restaurants and entertainment facilities. Famous attractions such as Wuyi Square, Huangxing Road Pedestrian Street and the Fire Palace are all located in the Tianxin District. Choosing to stay here allows visitors to enjoy easy access to transportation and a wealth of commercial facilities.

Kaifu District

Kaifu District is located in the northern part of Changsha City, adjacent to the Xiangjiang River. This area is home to many parks and recreational areas such as the Changsha Zoo, Du Fujiang Pavilion and Baisha Ancient Well. Choosing to stay in the Kaifu District allows visitors to experience the tranquility and natural beauty of the city of Changsha.
