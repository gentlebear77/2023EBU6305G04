Changsha Technology New City

Changsha Technology New City is a planned urban area in Changsha, China, dedicated to the development of high-tech industries and businesses. It is located in the middle of the city, about 20 km from the city centre and is interconnected with the city's public transport system. This new technological city is built on a vast area of land, approximately 210 square kilometres, and is designed to accommodate a population of 25 million people. The city boasts advanced infrastructure such as state-of-the-art roads, bridges and power plants, as well as state-of-the-art industries such as biotechnology, information technology and advanced manufacturing.
