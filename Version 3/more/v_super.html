Super Wen and You

Super Wen and You is a food amusement park with lobster as its main focus, and multiple concepts such as experience, culture and creativity. It focuses on the creation of scenic spaces, with nostalgia as its theme, presenting the original Changsha city life. The old building contains nearly 100 households and more than 20 shops, making it a super community within a building of old Changsha in the 1980s, restoring the culture and sentiment of old Changsha in all aspects.
