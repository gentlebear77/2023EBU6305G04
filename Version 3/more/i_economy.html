Changsha Economic and Technological Development Zone

The Changsha Economic Development Zone is a designated area in the city of Changsha, China, that is dedicated to the development of high-tech industries and businesses. The zone is located in the central part of the city, approximately 20 kilometers away from the city center, and is interconnected with the city's public transportation system. The economic development zone is home to a number of major companies, including Huawei, ZTE, Loral Space & Communications, and China Mobile. The zone has also received significant investment from foreign companies, such as Samsung, Sony, and NTT Data.
