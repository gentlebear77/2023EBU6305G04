Changsha Meixihu International Cultural Arts Centre

Designed and built by Zaha Hadid Architects, the Changsha Meixihu International Cultural Arts Centre contains a Museum of Contemporary Art (MICA), a large theatre with a capacity of 1,800 and a multi-purpose hall (Little Theatre). Using the pedestrian circulation around the site as a design reference, the building is organically connected to the neighbourhood. Visitors can experience the atmosphere of the three courtyards inside the cultural centre as well as participate in outdoor activities while enjoying the sculpture exhibition.
