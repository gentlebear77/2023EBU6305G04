Changsha Economic and Technological Development Zone

The Mayor's Office of Changsha City is a government agency responsible for promoting economic development and planning in the city. It has a busy schedule of meetings, workshops, and consultations with various business and industry associations to promote the development of the city. The office also oversees the implementation of policies and programs to support the growth of the local economy, and works closely with other government agencies to ensure a smooth and efficient implementation of policies and programs.
