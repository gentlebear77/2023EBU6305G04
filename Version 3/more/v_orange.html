Orange Island

Orange Island, located in the centre of the Xiangjiang River in the Yuelu district of Changsha, is the largest of the many alluvial sandbars in the lower reaches of the river and is known as 'China's First Island'. From south to north, it runs across the heart of the river, looking west towards Mount Yuelu and east towards Changsha City, surrounded by water on all sides, stretching for more than ten miles, about 40 metres across at the narrowest point and 140 metres across at the widest point, in the shape of a long island. Orange Island has attractions such as the Mao Zedong Youth Art Sculpture and the Ask the Sky Terrace. Thousands of species of flowers, plants and vines grow in the landscape, including 143 species of valuable plants. There are also many rare animals such as gulls, foxes and badgers.
