Yuelu Mountain Scenic Area

Located in the Yuelu District of Changsha City, Hunan Province, Yuelu Mountain is 295.6 metres above sea level and covers an area of 35.20 square kilometres, the last of the 72 peaks of Mount Hengshan, one of the four major maple viewing destinations in China. There are four core scenic spots: Mount Lu, Orange Island, Yuelu Academy and the New People's Society. The scenic spots include the Yuelu Academy, the Ai Evening Pavilion, the Lushan Temple, the Yunlu Palace, the former site of the New People's Society, Huang Xing's Tomb, Cai E's Tomb and the former site of the wartime headquarters of the Ninth War Area Command.
