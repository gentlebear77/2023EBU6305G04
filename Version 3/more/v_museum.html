Hunan Provincial Museum

The Hunan Provincial Museum was founded in the early 1950s and is located in the Kaifu district of Changsha. The museum has a rich collection of cultural relics, especially the Mawangdui Han tomb relics, Shang and Zhou bronzes, Chu relics, ceramics, paintings and calligraphy and modern relics. It is the largest comprehensive history and art museum in Hunan Province, as well as a national excellent patriotic education demonstration base and one of the 4A-level tourist attractions in Hunan Province.
