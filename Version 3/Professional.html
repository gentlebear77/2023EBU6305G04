Advantageous Industries



Finance

The provincial government has made it clear that the province's efforts will be focused on building a financial centre in Hunan, having a good ecological environment for financial talents.

Technology

Hunan has a total of 19 State Key Laboratories and 14 State Research Centres, with a positive cycle of interaction between talents and research institutes for mutual achievement.

Manufacturing Industry

Relying on Sany Heavy Industry, Zoomlion Heavy Industry and other construction machinery industries, Changsha's industrial scale has ranked first in the country for ten consecutive years.




Travel

Changsha is rich in cultural tourism resources and has a high cultural value. It is the only historical city in China where the site of the city has not been moved for 3,000 years.

Catering

Changsha has a diverse catering industry, covering local specialties and cuisines from all over Hunan, and there is a huge market demand for catering waiter recruitment.

Entertainment

Changsha's entertainment industry is well-developed and leading in the country, with Changsha becoming the first city in China to be awarded the title of "Media Arts Capital" in the world.






Living Standards


• Changsha tops the list of popular cities to live in in 2022.

• Over the past decade or so, Changsha's house prices have maintained a slow pace of growth and are still at 12,000 per square foot.

• Changsha has the highest proportion of people in cities with more than five hours of leisure time per day (19.83%), with a growing demand for leisure and entertainment spending.

• More young people are finding a sense of belonging in the city and are enjoying the sense of moderation that Changsha offers.
