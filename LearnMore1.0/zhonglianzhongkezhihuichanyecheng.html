Zhonglian Heavy Industry Group

Zhonglian Heavy Industry Group Co., Ltd., also known as ZHLGJ, is a leading Chinese industrial enterprise that specializes in the production and sale of construction machinery, including loaders, dumpers, and crushers. The company is headquartered in Zhuhai, Guangdong Province, and has a global presence in more than 100 countries. ZHLGJ has recently developed an Intelligent Industry City, which is a unique business ecosystem that combines the strengths of the company's products, services, and partnerships with those of other leading companies in the industry The Smart Industry City aims to create a sustainable and innovative business environment that enables ZHLGJ and its partners to achieve greater success and growth
